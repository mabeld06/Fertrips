BUCKINGHAM PALACE

The Buckingham Palace is the London home of Queen Elizabeth II, this is a place that you most go if you are in London. On the tour, you'll have access to the 19 State Rooms where the queen and members of the royal family host guests for state, ceremonial and official affairs,these rooms display some of the most magnificent pieces from the Royal Collection.
